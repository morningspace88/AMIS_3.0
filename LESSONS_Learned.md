# LESSONS LEARNED

## The flaw

The filtering in AMIS 3.0 works. But behind it — emptiness. A person who fails the verification receives a rejection and has no clear path to improve and return.

## Why it happened

I focused too much on protecting against destructive people and overlooked that even a person who makes a mistake deserves a chance.

## What's next

Work is underway to create a more balanced and fair system where punishment is not the only answer.

AMIS should not only filter but also give an opportunity to return to those who are willing to change.
